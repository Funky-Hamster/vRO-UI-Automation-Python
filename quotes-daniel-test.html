Filter Using Queries

You can do OR inside a filter statement using commas, e.g.:

Tags: tagA, tagB

You can do AND with two filter statements, e.g.:

Tags: tagA
,
Tags: tagB

You can filter using a specific field or attribute, e.g.:

Plugin: vCenter
or
Status: Running
